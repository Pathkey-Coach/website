values:
  - name: "Stewardship"
    content: |
      We treat your business as if it were our own. Not because we're pretending — but because that's what partnership means. Every recommendation, every decision, every line of code we touch is weighed against a simple question: is this genuinely good for the organisation and its people? Leadership is stewardship, not status. The title on the card matters less than whether people and customers are genuinely better off.
  - name: "Clarity"
    content: |
      We cut through the noise. AI is drowning in hype, buzzwords, and vendor pitches designed to confuse. We don't add to that. We say what we mean, explain what we don't know, and help you see clearly — so you can make decisions with confidence, not anxiety. No jargon for the sake of jargon. No complexity for the sake of looking clever.
  - name: "Empathy"
    content: |
      We understand before we advise. Your context, your constraints, your people — they all matter. We don't walk in with a template or a pre-built playbook. We listen first. Because the right answer for one organisation is the wrong answer for another, and the only way to know the difference is to genuinely understand where you are.
  - name: "Responsibility"
    content: |
      We own what we do. If something doesn't work, we say so. If we've made a mistake, we fix it. If the honest answer is "you don't need us for this," we'll tell you that too. We'd rather lose an engagement than lose your trust. Responsibility means being accountable for outcomes, not just deliverables.
