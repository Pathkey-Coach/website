How to maintain equilibrium between the three critical pillars of any sustainable business.

Originally published on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/triangle-balancing-people-customers-profits-suyog-patki-05eff).

<!--more-->

The triangle of People, Customers, and Profits isn't a trade-off – it's a balance. When you invest in your people, they take care of your customers. When customers are genuinely served, profits follow. The mistake is optimising for one at the expense of the others.
